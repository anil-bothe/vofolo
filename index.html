What is GI tag Products

Government of India announced some products are very specials from a specific Geographical location it's included Handicrafts , Agriculture , Food stuff , Manufacture and Natural Goods. A Geographical Indication (GI) is a name or sign used on certain products which corresponds to a specific Geographical location or region (e.g. a town , region , or country). India as a member of WTO , enacted the Geographical Indication of Goods (Registration and Protection) Act, 1999 has come into force with effect from 15 September 2003. GIs have been defined under Article 22(1) of the WTO Agreement on Trade-Related Aspects of Intellectual Property Rights (TRIPS) Agreement.
